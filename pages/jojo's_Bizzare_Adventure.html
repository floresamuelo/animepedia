Summary: JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a TV series and manga that tells the story of the Joestar family, who have psychic abilities and must use them to fight supernatural enemies. The story is told in parts, each following a different member of the Joestar family, who often have names that can be shortened to "JoJo".s
