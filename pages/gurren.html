Summary: Gurren Lagann is a 2007 TV series that takes place in a fictional future where the Spiral King, Lordgenome, rules Earth and forces humans to live in underground villages. The story follows two teenagers, Simon and Kamina, who live in one of these villages and want to go to the surface. Simon discovers a device and they use a mecha called Lagann to reach the surface, where they fight against Lordgenome's forces alongside other humans.
