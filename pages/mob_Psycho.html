Summary: The series follows Kageyama Shigeo, an eighth grader with psychic abilities who goes by the nickname "Mob". Mob wants to live a normal life and keep his powers under control, but he often gets into trouble and struggles to express himself. To help him learn to control his abilities, Mob works as an assistant to Arataka Reigen, a self-proclaimed spirit medium with no psychic powers.
