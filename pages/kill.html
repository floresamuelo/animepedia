Summary: Ryuko Matoi, a transfer student who seeks revenge for her father's murder. Ryuko's search leads her to Honnouji Academy, where the student council, led by Satsuki Kiryuin, use powerful uniforms called Goku Uniforms to rule the school and city with fear. The uniforms give the student council superhuman abilities, which they use to control the rest of the students and staff.
